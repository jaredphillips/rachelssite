I love making terrariums. I can’t stop making terrariums. My home is becoming one big terrarium. Time to share these little creations...with you!

Terrariums provide a platform upon which my idealistic dreams can come true! Taking pieces from the environment at large and manipulating them into a small container where they will thrive and establish their own, separate environment is fascinating and inspiring to me. An interesting terrarium is one that considers both form and function, one that is capable of sustaining life, both real and imagined….making that happen is the fun part!
